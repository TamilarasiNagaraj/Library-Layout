Description

This comprehensive volume traces the development of modern physics, from the groundbreaking discoveries of the late 19th century to the cutting-edge research of today. It delves into the key theories and experiments that have shaped our understanding of the universe, including quantum mechanics, relativity, and particle physics.

The book also examines the contributions of prominent physicists and the societal impact of their work. Ideal for students and researchers alike, this book provides a thorough and engaging overview of the field.
